New Ways to Move

Re:Mobilize introduces three movement-based gameplay mechanics, each of which offers a totally new way to interact with the Quake world, opening up devious new challenges.

Perform death-defying leaps of faith across even the most intimidating gaps and chasms with trampolines.

Nothing can stop you, not even gravity itself. More than just simple ladders, wiremeshes let you move up, down, sideways, and even hang from ceilings.

The lighthook's purely photonic composition allows it to instantly latch onto any visible lightpanel, regardless of distance. You'll be hooked in no time - literally.
